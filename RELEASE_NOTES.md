# SiriBot v1.0.0 - First Production Release

We're excited to announce the first production release of SiriBot, your personal AI assistant for macOS!

## What is SiriBot?

SiriBot is an easy-to-use AI assistant that lives in your menu bar and helps you accomplish tasks on your Mac — with just your voice or keyboard. All AI processing happens locally on your machine, ensuring your privacy is never compromised.

## Key Features

### 🤖 Always There
- Lives in your menu bar, ready when you need it
- Start chatting instantly with voice or keyboard
- Works alongside any app

### 🗣️ Voice Control
- "Hey SiriBot, organize my Downloads folder"
- Hands-free operation while you work
- Configurable wake word

### 📅 Apple Integration
- **Calendar** — Create events, see your schedule
- **Notes** — Save notes, search your notes
- **Reminders** — Set and manage reminders
- **Shortcuts** — Works with Apple Shortcuts app

### 🔄 Handoff Mode
Let SiriBot work on its own virtual desktop while you continue working.

### 🌐 Multi-Device Sync
Sync your settings across all your Macs.

## Privacy First

Your data stays on your Mac:
- All AI processing happens locally by default
- No subscription required
- No cloud accounts needed
- Your conversations are private

## System Requirements

- macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac
- 4GB RAM minimum
- Ollama (free, runs locally)

## Getting Started

1. **Download Ollama** from [ollama.com](https://ollama.com)
2. **Install a model**: `ollama pull llama3.2`
3. **Download SiriBot** from the DMG installer in this release
4. **Launch SiriBot** and follow the setup wizard
