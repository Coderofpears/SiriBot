# SiriBot v1.0.0 Marketing Plan

## Product Positioning

**SiriBot** - Your Personal AI Assistant for Mac that respects your privacy

### Key Messaging
- Privacy-first AI assistant that runs entirely on your Mac
- No cloud accounts, no subscriptions, no data leaving your machine
- Powerful local AI with Ollama integration
- Seamless Apple integration (Calendar, Notes, Reminders, Shortcuts)
- Voice control and menu bar convenience

### Unique Value Proposition
Unlike other AI assistants that send your data to the cloud, SiriBot processes everything locally on your Mac. You get the power of AI without compromising your privacy.

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience

- Developers and power users
